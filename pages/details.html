How to Get Here?

You can fly to Athens and spend a couple of days there visiting the sites. See the Acropolis, the Acropolis Museum and walk around all the beautiful neighborhoods.

Then, there are three different routes you can take:

  • City by the Lake Route: Our first suggestion is that you take a plane to Ioannina and then drive from there to Parga. If you have time, you could spend a day or two in Ioannina, which is a beautiful city by a lake full of ancient history, and/or drive up to the Zagorohoria region, with its precious villages, gorgeous mountains and pristine rivers.

  • The Venetian Coast Route: You could also fly through Preveza and then drive from there to Parga. This way you could enjoy the beauty of the western coast of Greece, which was once under Venetian control, and visit beautiful beaches and great architecture.

  • The Road Trip: Finally, if you enjoy driving and want to visit great sites on your way to Parga, you could rent a car in Athens and just drive; it takes 5 to 6 hours but it's a pretty awesome trip. On your way to the wedding you could stop by Corinth, make a detour to beautiful Nafplion and Epidavros, cross the cool bridge near Patras, take in all the great sites by the coast, and visit Preveza.
