How to Get Here?

You can fly to Athens and spend a couple of days there visiting the sites. See the Acropolis, the Acropolis Museum and walk around all the beautiful neighborhoods.

Then, you can either fly or drive. If you prefer to fly, we suggest you take a plane to Ioannina and then drive from there to Parga. Alternatively, you could also fly to Preveza and drive from there.

If you'd rather drive all the way, you can rent a car in Athens and go by road to Parga. It takes 5 to 6 hours but it's a pretty awesome drive and you can stop in many places along the way.
